About

I help organizations modernize legacy software, improve performance, and deliver more through deep engineering experience and AI-driven execution.

I have spent the last 20 years building enterprise software across government, healthcare, financial services, and large corporate environments. My work today focuses on modernization, architecture, performance, and practical delivery.

My story

From software engineer to modernization specialist.

I started in small teams building software for local government contracts. Over time, I moved into larger organizations and more demanding enterprise environments, working on complex internal platforms, business-critical systems, and award-winning software.

That path pushed me beyond implementation and into architecture, technical leadership, and high-stakes problem solving. Today, I help companies modernize legacy .NET systems, stabilize production software, improve performance, and use AI and automation to multiply engineering output.

Working principles

  • Modernize where it creates lasting leverage.
  • Reduce complexity before adding more software.
  • Performance and maintainability are business features.
  • Automation should remove waste, not add noise.
  • AI is a force multiplier when used with discipline.
